Robotics & STEM Education

Empowering the next generation through embedded systems and intelligent robotics.

Teaching 500+ Students

Teaching robotics to over 500 students has been one of my most rewarding experiences. I learned quickly that each student understands logic and mechanics differently. Some are highly visual, needing to see a breadboard circuit working before the code makes sense, while others grasp the programming flow first and then connect the hardware.

My core curriculum centered around Arduino, teaching the fundamentals of sensors, motors, and microcontrollers. We built practical, hands-on projects that demystified technology. Because of the rapid advancement in tech, I have taken a strong interest in learning to teach Raspberry Pi and Raspberry Pi Pico to introduce AI, Computer Vision, and more advanced embedded concepts into the mix.

DASAFA Robotics Champions

A major highlight of my teaching career was tutoring my students who participated in DASAFA (The District Agricultural Show and Science Fair), a prestigious regional STEM and agricultural exhibition in Zimbabwe that challenges students to solve real-world community problems. Under my mentorship, our team secured First Place in the Robotics Category. The winning submissions featured an automated Smart Farm and an autonomous Object Avoidance Rover, showcasing their incredible ability to apply complex logic and electronics to solve real-world problems.
